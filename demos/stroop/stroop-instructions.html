Stroop Test Instructions

In this test, you will see a word displayed on the screen. The word will be the name of a color, but the font color of the word may not match the word itself.

Your task is to identify the color of the font, not the word itself. For example, if the word "Red" is displayed in blue font, the correct answer is "Blue."

Use the following keys on your keyboard to select the color:

  • R for Red
  • G for Green
  • B for Blue
  • Y for Yellow
  • P for Purple

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible. The test will last for 30 seconds.
