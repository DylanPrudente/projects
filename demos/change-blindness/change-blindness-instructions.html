Change Blindness Task Instructions

In this task, you will see two similar images alternating. Your goal is to spot the difference between the two images.

The task will progress through three stages:

  • Stage 1: Images alternate with a 1-second buffer.
  • Stage 2: Images alternate with a 0.2-second buffer.
  • Stage 3: Images alternate with no buffer.

Click "Start Task" to begin the task.
