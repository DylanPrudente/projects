Ebbinghaus Illusion

The Ebbinghaus illusion involves two circles of the same size, each surrounded by different-sized circles. The central circle surrounded by smaller circles appears larger than the one surrounded by larger circles. This illusion highlights the influence of relative size and contextual comparison in visual perception.
